steak


Description

Today's recipe is a juicy pan seared ribeye steak. This steak is easy to cook and
only takes about 5 minutes to cook. All the ingrdients used in this dish can easily be bought


Ingredients


  • 1 ribeye steak
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • rosemary or thyme
  • cooking oil
  • kosher salt & black pepper

Steps


  1. Heat cast iron pan on high heat until just beginning to smoke
  2. Season steak with salt and pepper
  3. Add a high smoke point oil like avocado oil or vegetable oil
  4. Now add the steak to the pan, laying it away from you
  5. Cook the steak for 2 minutes on one side
  6. immediately after flipping, add the butter, garlic and thyme
  7. From now until it is finished cooking for 2 minutes, you want to take a spoon and baste the steak with the melted butter
  8. When it's done, remove the steam from the pan and let it sit for 15 minutes before cutting it
  9. Done!
