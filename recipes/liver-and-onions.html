Liver and Onions



Description

In this recipe we are going to cook a British staple: Liver and Onions
Liver is extremely cheap and high in nutrients and protein. Eat liver when you are poor.

Ingredients

  • Liver
  • Onions
  • Butter
  • Salt, pepper, green seasoning, paprika, cinnamon,
    geera powder, garlic powder, bbq sauce.

Steps

  1. Chop onions long and thin
  2. Add butter to medium heat pan
  3. when butter is melted add the onions
  4. Caramelize the onions until golden brown
  5. In a next pan add some butter
  6. When butter melts add the liver
  7. Cook for about 15 minutes
  8. Done!
