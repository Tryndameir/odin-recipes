Egg

Description

We are making a famous breakfast called egg and bacon. This healthy,
high protein and no carbohydrate meal is top of the line and clean

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • onions
  • butter or olive oil
  • bacon
  • powered seasoning
  • sausages
  • pementoes

Steps

  1. heat butter or olive oil in a pan on low heat
  2. Add the onions and bacon when the butter is melted
  3. caramelize the onions and bacon
  4. add the sausages
  5. Now chop the pementoes and season and beat the 4 eggs
  6. When sausages are cooked add the egg to the pan and scramble
  7. when the egg is almost done add the pementoes
  8. Done!
